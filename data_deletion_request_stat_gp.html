Data Deletion Request – Shopping List STAT Last updated: 10 December 2025 This page explains how users of Shopping List STAT (developed by Alex STA ) can request permanent deletion of personal data that may be stored off your device , such as crash reports and support e-mails. The app does not create user accounts and does not use advertising SDKs or analytics platforms. The only data stored outside your device are: - crash and performance logs in Firebase Crashlytics ; and - e-mails and attachments that you send to our support address. All lists, notes, voice recordings, and photos you create inside the app are stored only on your device and are not collected by us. --- How to submit a data deletion request 1. Compose an e-mail Send an e-mail to: [head.stat@gmail.com](mailto:head.stat@gmail.com) Use the subject line: > “Data Deletion Request – Shopping List STAT” 2. Provide information to help us locate your data Please include as much of the following as possible, so we can find and delete the relevant records: - Your device model (for example: “Samsung Galaxy A52”). - Your Android version (for example: “Android 14”). - The app version of Shopping List STAT (from the app’s Settings or from Google Play). - Approximate date and time (with time zone) of: - your most recent use of the app, and/or - the most recent crash you experienced. - The e-mail address you used if you previously contacted our support. - Any error IDs or references you received from our support (if applicable). We understand that you may not know every detail — provide what you can. We will use this information to search for related records in Firebase Crashlytics and in our support mailbox. 3. (Optional) Specify the scope of deletion In your e-mail, you may specify what should be erased: - All data stored off-device; - Crash logs only (Firebase Crashlytics); - Support messages only (e-mails and attachments). If you do not specify, we will treat your request as “delete all personal data stored off-device for this app”. 4. Identity verification To protect your privacy, we may request additional details only to verify that the request relates to your own data (for example, confirming device model or prior support messages). We will not use this information for any other purpose. 5. Confirmation and timeline - We will confirm receipt of your request within 7 days . - Once we have verified your request, we will start the deletion process. - Deletion will normally be completed within 30 days of confirmation, and we will notify you when it is finished. --- What will be deleted (within 30 days of a verified request) After we verify your request, we will delete or request deletion of the following data associated with your use of Shopping List STAT : 1. Crash and performance logs (Firebase Crashlytics) This includes, to the extent they can be associated with your request: - crash reports and stack traces; - app version, device model, OS version; - technical identifiers used by Crashlytics (for example, Crashlytics Installation UUID, Firebase Installation ID); - limited network and region information (for example, IP-derived country or region). Firebase Crashlytics typically retains crash reports and related identifiers for about 90 days before removing them from live and backup systems according to its own retention policy. ([Firebase][2]) After we process your request, we will: - delete or anonymize any matching crash data that we can control; and - submit the necessary deletion actions within Firebase Crashlytics for the relevant installation identifiers and crash sessions, where technically possible. 2. User-support data (e-mails and attachments) - Your e-mails to [head.stat@gmail.com](mailto:head.stat@gmail.com) relating to Shopping List STAT; - any attachments (screenshots, logs, or files) you sent us. These e-mails are stored on secure servers of our e-mail provider (Google). We will delete: - the support thread(s) relating to your request; and - any associated attachments. In our standard practice, support messages are deleted no later than 180 days after the ticket is closed. If you request deletion earlier, we will erase them sooner as part of your data deletion request. 3. Other server-side technical logs (if any) If we happen to maintain any additional server logs that could reasonably identify you (for example, temporary security logs linked to your support interactions), we will remove or anonymize these records as part of the deletion process. --- Third-party processors we will notify on your behalf As part of fulfilling your data deletion request, we will take reasonable steps to ensure that third-party processors used by Shopping List STAT delete or anonymize corresponding data. Currently, the only such processor that may receive data from the app is: - Firebase Crashlytics (Google LLC) – crash reporting and diagnostics. Where technically possible, we will submit deletion or removal actions within the Firebase Crashlytics console for data associated with your request. --- Local data on your device The Application stores the following only on your device : - shopping lists and items; - text notes; - voice notes and audio recordings; - photos attached to items or notes; - local settings and preferences. These data are not collected by us and are not transmitted off your device. Because of this, we cannot delete them remotely. To remove these data from your device, you can: - Clear app data on Android: > Settings → Apps → Shopping List STAT → Storage → Clear data or - Uninstall the app : > Settings → Apps → Shopping List STAT → Uninstall Both actions remove local app data from your device. --- What may be retained In some cases, certain information may remain only in aggregated or anonymized form that no longer allows us (or our providers) to identify you, for example: - aggregated crash statistics; - high-level metrics (such as total number of crashes per app version). Such aggregated data may be retained for lawful purposes such as: - maintaining service stability and security; - understanding overall app performance; - meeting legal or regulatory requirements. --- Questions? If you have any questions about the deletion process or our privacy practices, please contact us at: [head.stat@gmail.com](mailto:head.stat@gmail.com) You can also find general guidance about account and data deletion requirements in the Google Play policy resources .
