App name: Shopping List STA
Short: Smart & simple grocery lists with categories, prices, and dark mode.
Full:
Shopping List STA helps you plan shopping quickly and stay organized.
Contains ads.

Features
- Unlimited lists
- Categories & filters — items are grouped for quicker shopping
- Budget tracking — enter prices and see the running total
- Recipes → add all ingredients to a list in one tap
- Notes with photos
- Dark and light themes
- Language and currency options
- Import items from text or the clipboard

Designed for everyday use: fast item entry, clear totals, and a clean interface that works offline.
